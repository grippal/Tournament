Standings

Logistics

  • $50 a team
  • Schedule will be posted by Friday
  • Games will be played on Saturday Morning with the 1st game beginning at 11:00 am
  • Tiebreaker is head to head record
  • OT is a diamond rack, no balls back

Scoring

  • 3 pts for a win
  • 1 pts for an OT loss
  • 0 pts for regulation Loss
  • (-1) pts if you lose and have >= 5 cups remaining
  • (-3) pts if you lose and have >= 10 cups remaining

Rules

  • Eye for an eye determines who starts with the balls
  • 15 cup beer pong with cups filled halfway
  • 1 Deathcup
  • 2 Balls
  • 1 Rerack per game nand gentlemans
  • Balls back if partners both sink their cups
  • Fingering is allowed
  • Island is allowed with >= 4 cups left
  • Heating up and Fire MUST be called
  • Bounce = Two Cups
  • If ball returns to shooter he may shoot behind the back
  • Watch your elbows (Will)
  • Redemption is allowed
  • Losing team drinks the cups they did not hit

Scheduling Conflicts

  • If you cant make the game you have two opportunities to pick a sub
  • Make up games are allowed if teams come to an agreement
  • (-2) pts for not showing up to a game without notifying the commish and the other team gets an auto win
